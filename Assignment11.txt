1. What is data concatination in pandas ?
A. Combining two or more dataframes into a single dataframe is known as Data 
   concatenation in pandas.
2. Types of data concatination in pandas?
A. There are two types of concatenations available in pandas:
   1.concating the dataframes one by one (axis=0)
   2.concating the dataframes side by side (axis=1)
3. What are the functions available for data concatination/append in pandas?
A. There are two functions available for data concatination/append in pandas are 
   1. append()
      syntax:-
        df1.append(df2,ignore_index=True/False (False by default),axis=0/1 (0 by 
        default),sort=True/false (False by default))
   2. concat()
      syntax:-
        pd.concat([df1,df2,....,dfn],axis=0/1(0 by default),ignore_index=True/False(False by 
        default),keys=[key1,key2,key3,...,keyn])
